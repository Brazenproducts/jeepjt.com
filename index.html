Complete Jeep Gladiator JT Accessories Guide — From Cab to Truck Bed

The JT is a Jeep that's also a truck. Its accessory landscape spans both worlds. Here's everything that matters.

The complete JT guide: The Jeep Gladiator JT is a genuinely unique vehicle — a capable Jeep with a real truck bed. Accessories span from traditional Jeep trail gear to truck bed utility equipment. This guide covers the full JT accessory landscape from cab to tailgate.

What Makes the Jeep Gladiator JT Different

The Jeep Gladiator JT launched for 2019, becoming the first factory Jeep truck since the Scrambler CJ-8 ended production in 1985. It's built on the JL Wrangler platform with a purpose-engineered truck body extension. Key specs that affect accessory choices:

  • Wheelbase: 137.3" (substantially longer than any Wrangler)
  • Bed length: 60" (standard) with steel floor
  • Payload capacity: Up to 1,700 lbs depending on trim
  • Tow capacity: Up to 7,650 lbs (V6) or 6,000 lbs (EcoDiesel)
  • Front cab architecture: Identical to JL Wrangler from windshield forward

That last point matters for accessory shopping — any JL Wrangler cab accessory (windshield shade, roll bar upgrades, grab handles, seat covers) fits the JT cab. Truck bed accessories are unique to the JT.

Bed Liners

Factory JTs come with either a plastic drop-in liner or a spray-in bedliner (dealer-applied option). Owners who skip the factory liner add aftermarket spray-in or drop-in liners. For work truck JTs, spray-in (Rhino or LINE-X) provides the most durable protection with no lift risk for water/debris collection.

Bed Racks and Overland Systems

The JT's bed serves as the platform for elaborate overlanding setups. Bed rack systems from manufacturers like Expedition One and Victory 4x4 allow rooftop tent mounting, lighting towers, and accessory mounting while preserving bed access below.

Frequently Asked Questions — Jeep Gladiator JT Accessories

Frequently Asked Questions — Jeep Gladiator JT Accessories

Do JL Wrangler accessories fit the Gladiator JT?

Cab-forward accessories (windshield shades, roll bar handles, seat covers, A-pillar mounts) generally fit both JL and JT because they share the same cab architecture. Truck bed accessories are unique to the JT. Always confirm JT-specific compatibility for any accessory.

What's the payload capacity of the Gladiator JT?

Payload varies by trim and configuration: Sport with V6 is rated around 1,600 lbs; Rubicon around 1,000 lbs due to heavier standard equipment. Always check your specific VIN's payload sticker on the driver's door jamb.

Is the Gladiator JT worth buying as a daily driver work truck?

For owners who need genuine off-road capability combined with a truck bed, the JT is essentially unmatched in its class. The compromises are cost (premium vs competing trucks), longer wheelbase (reduced off-road articulation vs Wrangler), and interior space similar to Wrangler rather than full-size truck.

What size bed cover fits the Gladiator JT?

The Gladiator JT has a 5-foot (60-inch) bed. Unlike most trucks where multiple bed lengths exist, all JTs sold in the US have the same 60" bed — there's no short or long bed variant to specify.

Can I put a rooftop tent on a Gladiator JT?

Yes — the JT is one of the most popular rooftop tent platforms. Bed rack systems allow RTT mounting while preserving bed access. The JT's payload capacity (1,000–1,700 lbs depending on trim) comfortably supports most RTT systems plus occupants.

What's the best lift kit for a stock Gladiator JT?

A 2.5" lift is the most recommended starting point for JT owners — enough to run 35" tires without rubbing, maintains acceptable highway manners, and doesn't require re-gearing at the stock 3.73 (Sport) or 4.10 (Rubicon) axle ratio.

Are there JT-specific seat covers that accommodate the factory MOLLE seat backs?

Yes. Bartact makes JT-specific covers that work with factory MOLLE seat backs. Some owners replace factory MOLLE seats with full Bartact covers including MOLLE front panels — a clean upgrade that also adds protection the factory MOLLE doesn't provide.

Can the Gladiator JT tow with a tonneau cover on?

Yes. Most hard and soft tonneau covers don't affect towing ability. The JT's factory tow rating (7,650 lbs with V6, 6,000 lbs with diesel) is independent of bed cover configuration. Ensure the hitch mount and wiring are accessible with the cover design you choose.
